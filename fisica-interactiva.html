Teaching Resources in Physics

Explore fundamental concepts in physics with examples, equations, and interactive simulations.

1. Projectile Motion

Understand the physics of a projectile's trajectory under the influence of gravity.

Equations:

Displacement in \(x\): \(x(t) = v_0 \cos(\theta) t\)

Displacement in \(y\): \(y(t) = v_0 \sin(\theta) t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2\)

Range: \(R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}\)

Explore Interactive Example

2. Harmonic Oscillator

Discover the periodic motion of a mass-spring system.

Equations:

Position: \(x(t) = A \cos(\omega t + \phi)\)

Angular frequency: \(\omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}\)

Explore Interactive Example

3. Electric Field of a Point Charge

Learn how electric fields are generated by point charges.

Equations:

Electric Field: \(\vec{E} = \frac{k_e q}{r^2} \hat{r}\)

Explore Interactive Example

4. Wave Interference

Explore the phenomenon of constructive and destructive interference of waves.

Equations:

Resultant Wave: \(y(t) = y_1(t) + y_2(t)\)

Wave Equation: \(y(t) = A \cos(kx - \omega t)\)
